About SprintPilot

Our journey to revolutionize project management for development teams worldwide.

Our Story

How SprintPilot came to be and the problems we set out to solve

The Idea

2023

Founded by a team of developers frustrated with existing project management tools, SprintPilot was conceived to create a solution specifically designed for software development workflows.

Launch

2024

After a year of development and beta testing with select teams, we officially launched SprintPilot with our core feature set focused on Git integration and real-time collaboration.

Growth

2025

SprintPilot has grown to support thousands of development teams across 40+ countries, with new features being developed based on direct community feedback.

Our Mission

What drives us forward every day

Developer-First

We build for developers, by developers. Every feature is designed with the software development workflow in mind, integrating seamlessly with the tools you already use.

Speed & Efficiency

We believe development teams should focus on building great products, not managing project tools. SprintPilot automates and simplifies the busywork of project management.

Meaningful Collaboration

Great software is built by teams working together effectively. We're dedicated to making collaboration natural, real-time, and productive for teams of all sizes.
